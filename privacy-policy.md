# Privacy Policy for Mend

**Last updated: July 7, 2026**

Mend ("we", "our", or "the app") is a personal wellness app designed to help you track your mood, journal your thoughts, and receive gentle AI-powered reflections. Your privacy is fundamental to how this app was built.

---

## 1. What Data We Collect

### Data stored only on your device
The following is stored locally using Apple's SwiftData framework and **never leaves your device** unless explicitly described below:

- **Your name or nickname** — entered during onboarding
- **Profile photo** — chosen from your photo library
- **Mood check-ins** — mood selections and any accompanying notes you write
- **Journal entries** — written journal entries and voice transcriptions
- **App preferences** — your healing focus, reminder time, and display settings

### Data sent to a third-party service
When you use the AI features (mood reflections, chat support), the following is sent to **OpenAI, LLC** to generate a response:

- Aggregated mood counts (e.g. "Calm: 3, Anxious: 2") — not raw text
- Your nickname (for personalised responses)
- Chat messages you type in the Talk to Mend screen
- Brief journal context when you start a chat from a reflection

This data is transmitted securely over HTTPS. We do not retain it on any server we operate. OpenAI's data practices are governed by [OpenAI's Privacy Policy](https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy).

### Data we never collect
- We do not collect your precise location
- We do not collect device identifiers or advertising IDs
- We do not use analytics SDKs or third-party tracking
- We do not have a backend server — there is no Mend account or login

---

## 2. How We Use Your Data

| Purpose | Data used | Stored where |
|---------|-----------|-------------|
| Display your name in greetings | Name / nickname | On your device |
| Show your mood history and trends | Mood entries | On your device |
| Generate AI mood reflections | Mood counts + name | Sent to OpenAI, not retained |
| AI chat support | Chat messages + name | Sent to OpenAI, not retained |
| Daily reminder notifications | Reminder time | On your device |
| Profile photo display | Photo data | On your device |

---

## 3. Third-Party Services

**OpenAI, LLC**
We use the OpenAI API to power the AI reflection and chat features. When you use these features, relevant data (described above) is sent to OpenAI's servers in the United States. OpenAI is subject to their own privacy policy. You can opt out of AI features by not using the "Talk to Mend" chat or by closing AI reflection prompts.

**Apple frameworks used (no data sharing)**
- SwiftData (local database)
- UserNotifications (local notifications only)
- Contacts framework (used only to place a phone call — no contact data is stored by Mend)
- PhotosUI (photo stays on device)
- Speech framework (transcription processed on-device by Apple)

## 4. Permissions We Request

| Permission | Why |
|-----------|-----|
| Microphone | To record voice journal entries |
| Speech Recognition | To transcribe voice journal recordings into text |
| Contacts | To let you call a friend or the crisis line from Calm Space |
| Photo Library | To let you set a profile photo |
| Notifications | To send you optional daily check-in reminders |

You can revoke any permission at any time in **Settings → Privacy & Security** on your iPhone.

---

## 5. Crisis Resources

Mend is a wellness support tool. It is **not** a crisis service and does not replace professional mental health care. If you are in crisis, please contact:

- **988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline** — call or text **988** (US, free, 24/7)
- **Crisis Text Line** — text HOME to **741741**
- **Emergency services** — call **911** (or your local equivalent)

---

## 6. Children's Privacy

Mend is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided data through this app, please contact us and we will take steps to remove it.

---

## 7. Data Retention & Deletion

All data stored locally on your device can be deleted at any time by:
- Removing your profile data via **Profile → Sign Out**
- Deleting the app from your device (this removes all SwiftData stored data)

We do not retain any data on servers we control. OpenAI's data retention is subject to their privacy policy.
