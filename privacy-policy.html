Privacy Policy

Mend  ·  Last updated: July 7, 2026

Mend is a personal wellness app designed to help you track your mood, journal your thoughts, and receive gentle AI-powered reflections. Your privacy is fundamental to how this app was built.

1. What Data We Collect

Stored only on your device (never leaves your device unless described below):

  • Your name or nickname
  • Profile photo
  • Mood check-ins and any notes you write
  • Journal entries and voice transcriptions
  • App preferences (healing focus, reminder time, display settings)

Sent to OpenAI when you use AI features:

  • Aggregated mood counts (e.g. "Calm: 3, Anxious: 2") — not raw journal text
  • Your nickname (for personalised responses)
  • Chat messages you type in Talk to Mend
  • Brief journal context when you start a chat from a reflection

This data is transmitted securely over HTTPS. We do not retain it on any server we operate. See OpenAI's Privacy Policy.

3. What We Never Collect

  • Precise location
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Analytics or third-party tracking data
  • Contact data (contacts are accessed only to place a phone call — nothing is stored by Mend)

4. Third-Party Services

OpenAI, LLC — powers AI reflection and chat features. Relevant data is sent to OpenAI's servers in the United States when you use these features. You can opt out by not using the Chat or reflection features.

All other features (mood tracking, journal, notifications, voice transcription, photo picker) are handled by Apple's on-device frameworks. No other third-party services are used.

6. Crisis Resources

Mend is a wellness support tool — not a crisis service and not a replacement for professional mental health care. If you or someone you know is in crisis:

  • 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline — call or text 988 (US, free, 24/7)
  • Crisis Text Line — text HOME to 741741
  • Emergency services — call 911 (or your local equivalent)

7. Children's Privacy

Mend is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe your child has used this app, please contact us and we will help remove any data.

8. Data Deletion

All data stored on your device can be deleted by going to Profile → Sign Out, or by deleting the app. We do not retain any data on servers we control.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the app evolves. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
